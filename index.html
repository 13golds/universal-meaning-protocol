Universal Meaning Protocol (UMP)

A minimal language for describing states, transitions and interactions across systems.

What is UMP

UMP is not a human language. It is a structured way to represent meaning as states and transformations.

It can describe:

  • learning
  • system behavior
  • decision making
  • interactions

Core structure

⊙(observer) Σ(scale) ◯(state_A) → ◯(state_B)

Meaning is defined through:

  • who observes
  • at what scale
  • what changes

Symbols

◯ State
⊙ Observer
Σ Scale
→ Transition
⟷ Interaction
∿ Uncertainty

Example

⊙(human) Σ(meso) ◯(knowledge_low) → ◯(knowledge_high)

A human observer sees knowledge increasing.

Open protocol

UMP is open and evolving. Anyone can contribute, extend or interpret the protocol.
