Universal Meaning Protocol

Universal Meaning Protocol (UMP) is an open protocol for describing meaning as states and transitions across different scales of reality.

Why UMP

Human languages are optimized for communication between humans. UMP is designed for structured representation of meaning, independent of culture, biology and spoken language.

Core entities

  • State – observable configuration of a system
  • Observer – reference point of interpretation
  • Scale – level of description
  • Transition – change between states
  • Interaction – coupling of systems
  • Uncertainty – limits of knowledge

Symbolic layer

Symbols are visual projections of structured meaning. They do not replace the formal model.

Open evolution

The protocol is designed to evolve through open versioning. All changes are recorded and discussed openly on GitHub.
